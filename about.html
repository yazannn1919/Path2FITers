About Path2FIT

A project built by students, for students

Path2FIT is a student-built platform created by Faculty of Information Technology students at ASU. Our goal is to help new and current students understand different majors, explore realistic paths, and feel confident about their academic and personal growth journey.

We built Path2FIT because we once faced the same uncertainty. Instead of confusion, we wanted clarity. Instead of guessing, we wanted guidance.

Our Mission

  • Make student paths clearer and less overwhelming
  • Provide honest guidance and realistic expectations
  • Encourage growth through self-assessment and exploration
  • Inspire confidence through real stories and experiences
